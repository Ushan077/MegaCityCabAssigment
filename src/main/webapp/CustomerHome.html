User Guide: Booking Management

1. Create a New Booking

Step 1: Auto-Generated Booking ID and Name

When you begin the booking process, a Booking ID is automatically generated for you. This ID is unique and allows you to track your booking. You will also be prompted to enter your Name.

Step 2: Enter Pickup Location

Select or enter your Pickup Location in the provided field. This is where your vehicle will pick you up.

Step 3: Enter Drop-off Location

In the Drop-off Location field, enter the destination where you want to be dropped off.

Step 4: Select Date

Choose the Date for your booking. You can select a date from the calendar, which will automatically update the booking details.

Step 5: Select Vehicle

Choose the Vehicle you would like to use for your trip from the available options. Each vehicle may have different pricing and features, so make your selection based on your needs.

Step 6: Confirm Booking

Once all details are filled in, click on the "Book Now" button to finalize your booking.

2. View Bill Information

After your booking is complete, you will have access to the Bill Information. To view it:

  • Navigate to the booking summary page.
  • Click on "Bill Information".
  • Here, you can see a breakdown of the total charges for your booking, including any additional fees or discounts.

3. Submit a Complaint

  • Locate the Complaints Section on your booking summary page.

  • Click on "Submit Complaint" to send your complaint to the support team.

4. View Responses to Complaints

Once your complaint is submitted, you will be able to view the response from the support team:

  • Under the Complaint Section, look for the Responses area.
  • The response will be displayed, including any actions taken by the support team to resolve your issue.
  • If further action is needed, you may be asked to provide additional information or clarification.
